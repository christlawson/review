Wine Categories

Discover the different styles of wine and what makes each unique.

Red Wine

Made from dark-colored grapes, red wines range from light and fruity to bold and full-bodied. Common varieties include Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, and Pinot Noir.

Food Pairing: Red meat, stews, and aged cheeses.

White Wine

Produced from green or yellow grapes, white wines are typically crisp and refreshing, with flavors ranging from citrus to tropical fruit.

Food Pairing: Seafood, poultry, and light pastas.

Sparkling Wine

Known for its bubbles, sparkling wine can be dry or sweet. Famous styles include Champagne, Prosecco, and Cava.

Food Pairing: Oysters, sushi, and festive appetizers.

Dessert Wine

Sweet wines served with desserts. Includes Port, Tokaji, and Sauternes. They are rich, aromatic, and perfect for indulgent pairings.

Food Pairing: Fruit tarts, blue cheese, chocolate.

Fortified Wine

Fortified with spirits like brandy, these wines include Sherry, Madeira, and Port. They are known for bold flavors and higher alcohol content.

Food Pairing: Nuts, dried fruits, and rich desserts.

Organic & Biodynamic Wine

Produced using sustainable farming practices, these wines avoid synthetic chemicals and embrace natural vineyard cycles.

Food Pairing: Farm-to-table dishes, vegetarian meals, fresh salads.
